FAQs regarding payment issues

Can I attempt a test multiple times?
A test can be attempted only once. However, you can refer to the solutions and analysis of the attempted tests as many times as you want.
Can I download the question paper with solutions after the test?
No, you cannot download the paper; however, you can access the paper and its solutions by logging into your account on the website or on the App.
Do you also provide solutions for the questions?
Yes, detailed solutions will be available to you after you finish the test. You will be able to access these solutions anytime after you have taken the test.
Will I get any report card after the test?
Yes, you will get a detailed analysis of your performance after submitting the test. You will also receive an email with your test report.
Till when can I access the solutions and analysis of my attempted tests?
To access solutions and analysis, you should have a valid Pass and test should not have expired. Attempted Tests will be available for review till 15 days after Test Expiry date.
I am not able to access My Tests, what should I do?
Mostly, your CS Testseries Pass has expired and you need to renew it. If that’s not the case, please write to admin@cstestseries.com
I am not able to see Tests for my desired exam. What should I do?
You can see all available test series for all exams in All Tests tab. Please ensure you are in the right course (course dropdown besides CS Testseries logo on top left corner). You will find your Tests in My Tests tab under respective Exam tabs. All your attempted tests should be available in Attempted Tests tab. If you are still not able to find your exam or tests, please write to admin@cstestseries.com
I do not have a computer, can I appear for the test through my phone?
Yes, you may appear for the test through your phone. Simply download the CS Testseries App on your Android phone and take the tests there.
What if my computer gets disconnected from internet during the test?
No need to panic. We auto-pause your test and you can resume the test from where you left. You can also manually pause a test by clicking on the 'Pause' icon on the top left corner inside the timer circle. Please note that these functionalities are not available in a Live Test. Kindly ensure that you have an uninterrupted internet and power connection during the Live Tests.
If I am unavailable on the day of Live Test, can I attempt the test on any other day?
No, you cannot attempt the test at any point other than the given time because appearing for the test at any other time will lose its value as a Live Test and it will be considered like any other practice test. Once the Live Test is over, you will be able to see the test along with its solutions.
I do not have online payment activated, can I use any other mode for payment?
You can use your Debit card, ATM card, Credit card, Netbanking account or payment wallets like Paytm/PayZapp/Freecharge/Ola Money to pay for the pass. If you do not have any of the above payment methods, send us an email at admin@cstestseries.com and we will suggest an alternative method.
My money got deducted twice while making the transaction, what should I do?
No need to worry! Generally, one of the transactions gets refunded back to your bank account automatically. In case this does not happen, you can reach out to us at admin@cstestseries.com and we will track your payment to get it refunded.
My transaction failed, but money got deducted from my account, what should I do?
In case you have entered a wrong email-id or phone number, please send us an email at admin@cstestseries.com with your order details, correct phone number, correct email id and scanned copy of your identity proof (driving license/voter card/aadhar card). We assure that we will get back to you.
I entered wrong email-id and/or phone number while making the transaction, what should I do?
In case you have entered a wrong email-id or phone number, please send us an email at admin@cstestseries.com with your order details, correct phone number, correct email id and scanned copy of your identity proof (driving license/voter card/aadhar card).
How can I apply for the refund?
To apply for a refund, you need to write an email to us at admin@cstestseries.com within 48 hours of purchase, mentioning in detail the reason for requesting a refund.
Will I get the refund if I want to stop using it before the Pass expiry?
Refund can be initiated only if requested within 48 hours of purchase of the Pass. Amount will be refunded if you have taken only Free Tests, and have not attempted any test(s) covered under Pass.
